Privacy Policy: Diddy
Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Data Collection and Processing
The Bot processes messages and commands within the Discord channels where it has been granted explicit permissions. This data is used solely to facilitate AI responses and automations.

2. Third-Party Service Providers
To generate responses, the Bot forwards prompts to third-party AI model providers (such as Anthropic, OpenAI, or DeepSeek) via the OpenClaw architecture. Use of this Bot is subject to the privacy policies of these underlying model providers.

3. Data Storage and Retention
Logs: Temporary logs may be stored on the operator's private VPS for debugging and performance monitoring.
No Sale of Data: No user data or message content is ever sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
Database: If the Bot utilizes a local SQLite database for session history, this data remains on the private host environment.
4. Data Deletion
Users may stop interacting with the Bot at any time. Upon request, the operator will manually purge specific logs or session data associated with a user, to the extent such data is technically retrievable.

5. Consent
By interacting with the Bot, you consent to the processing of your messages for the purpose of generating AI-driven utility and automation.
